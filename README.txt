INSTRUCTIONS TO RUN YOUR WEBSITE
================================

1. Open the "chemistry-website" folder on your Desktop.
2. Double-click "index.html" to open the website in your browser.

ADMIN SETUP
-----------
1. Register a new account with the phone number: 0701417838
2. This account will automatically get "Admin" privileges.
3. As Admin, you can:
   - Add/Delete Live Class links.
   - Upload Videos and Papers.
   - Approve student payments.

STUDENT ACCESS
--------------
1. Students register normally.
2. They cannot access Classes/Videos/Papers until they upload a payment slip.
3. Once a student uploads a slip:
   - A WhatsApp message is drafted to you.
   - You (as Admin) must login, go to the "Admin Panel" (on Dashboard), and click "Approve" for that student.
4. Once approved, the student can access the content.

DATA SAVING
-----------
All data (users, scheduled classes, videos) is saved in your browser's Local Storage. 
It will persist even if you close the browser, as long as you don't clear your browser cache.
